Cloudiness

This plot doesn't necessarily show any trend between latitude and cloudiness. We can't say cloudiness increase or decrease when latitude get closer to the equator. Clouds tend to form in abundance in the middle latitudes 60 degrees north and south of the equator. This is where the edges of polar and mid l atitude circulation cells collide and push air upward.
